Flying Wire provides professional aviation consulting for film or for large scale, Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) UAS applications.

In addition,we are cornering the market and using the most advanced solutions for Mining, Surveying, Mapping and Cinematography.

When it comes to saving time and providing a safe solution, you will be hard-pressed to find seasoned military aviators at your disposal, to fit any need.

Owner & Chief Pilot
Kelly J. Anthony

An Air Force Command Pilot with over 28 years Aviation experience. 19 year Military Veteran with thousands of hours of combat experience to include Predator and Reaper armed UAV’s or “Drones", and heavy KC-135 inflight refueling aircraft. At the rank of Major in the USAF, Kelly has held positions such as the Chief of Training. In this position he was in charge of developing and employing a new training program for all aircrew while transitioning to a new aircraft. Kelly has attended the Air Force Safety School in New Mexico and is currently the Squadron Assigned Flight Safety Officer. In 2009 Kelly was selected as the youngest military flight Instructor and one of only 6 pilot evaluators in his unit. Because of his extensive training and background in the Military, he has been able to lead Flying Wire through multiple projects in accordance with FAA Part 107 guidelines, utilizing Small Unmanned Air Systems (sUAS’s) and applying the deep knowledge required to accomplish each project with safety and efficiency.
